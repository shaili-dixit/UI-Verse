What Are Cookies?

Cookies are small text files stored on your device when you visit a website. They help websites remember user preferences, improve functionality and provide a better browsing experience.

UIverse uses cookies to enhance performance, improve security and personalize your experience across our platform.

Types of Cookies

Essential Cookies

Required for authentication, security and basic website functionality.

Analytics Cookies

Help us understand usage patterns and improve platform performance.

Preference Cookies

Save theme preferences, language settings and personalization options.

Marketing Cookies

Used to deliver relevant campaigns and measure advertising effectiveness.

How We Use Cookies

  • Improve website speed and performance.
  • Remember your preferences and settings.
  • Analyze visitor interactions and optimize UX.
  • Maintain security and protect user sessions.
