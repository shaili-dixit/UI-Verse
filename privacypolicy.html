Privacy Policy

We believe privacy is a right, not a feature. Here's exactly how we handle your data — transparently and responsibly.

01

Introduction

Welcome to UIverse. We are committed to protecting your personal information and your right to privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your data when you visit our website or use our UI component library.

By using UIverse, you agree to the collection and use of information in accordance with this policy. If you have any questions, please don't hesitate to contact us.

02

Information We Collect

We only collect information that is necessary to provide and improve our service. This may include:

Personal Identifiers

Name and email address when you sign up or contact us.

Usage Data

Pages visited, components viewed, time spent, and interaction patterns.

Device & Browser Info

Browser type, OS, screen resolution, and IP address for analytics.

03

How We Use Your Data

Your data is used solely to make UIverse better for you. Specifically, we use it to:

  • Improve website performance and user experience
  • Maintain and monitor our services for errors
  • Send optional updates about new components or features
  • Respond to support requests and feedback
  • Analyze usage trends to prioritize new features

We never sell, rent, or share your personal information with third parties for marketing purposes.

04

Cookies

We use cookies and similar tracking technologies to enhance your browsing experience. Here's what we use them for:

Type Purpose Duration
Essential Core site functionality (theme, session) Session
Analytics Understanding how pages are used 30 days
Preference Remembering dark mode, language, etc. 1 year

You can control cookies through your browser settings at any time.

05

Security

Protecting your data is a top priority. We use industry-standard practices to keep your information secure:

  • HTTPS encryption across the entire website
  • Secure servers with restricted access controls
  • Regular audits and vulnerability checks
  • Minimal data retention — we only keep what's necessary

While we take every reasonable precaution, no method of internet transmission is 100% secure. We will notify you promptly in the unlikely event of a data breach.

06

Third-Party Services

We may use trusted third-party tools to help operate our service. These partners may have access to your data only to the extent needed to perform their functions:

Google Analytics

For understanding traffic patterns and user behaviour.

GitHub Pages

Used to host and deploy the UIverse website.

Each third-party service is bound by their own privacy policies and we encourage you to review them.

07

Policy Updates

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time to reflect changes in our practices or for legal compliance. When we do, we will revise the "Last updated" date at the top of this page.

We encourage you to review this page periodically. Continued use of UIverse after any changes constitutes your acceptance of the updated policy.

Significant changes will always be communicated via a notice on our homepage or by email if you are subscribed.

08

Contact Us

If you have any questions, concerns, or requests regarding this Privacy Policy or the way we handle your data, please reach out — we're always happy to help.
